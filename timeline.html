Get to know the history of the one and only comet idol, Hoshimachi Suisei!

18/3/2018

Her Twitter account and YouTube channel were created on 18 March 2018. Her introduction video was uploaded four days later on 22 March.

21/6/2018

On 21 June, she debuted her second 2D costume. Like her first one, her movements and animations were limited. However, this costume was later updated to have much more movement, which included her hair and hat.
